## Core Thesis

1. **AGI by 2027** — Counting the OOMs (orders of magnitude) of compute scaling, algorithmic efficiency, and "unhobbling" gains makes AGI strikingly plausible within a few years.
2. **Intelligence explosion follows quickly** — Automated AI researchers (100M+ copies at 100x human speed) could compress a decade of algorithmic progress into a year, yielding superintelligence.
3. **Trillion-dollar clusters are coming** — AI revenue growth justifies massive capital mobilization; power (not chips) is the binding constraint.
4. **Lab security is critically inadequate** — Current AI labs have "random startup" security while building the most consequential technology in history.
5. **Alignment is unsolved but solvable** — Superalignment is a hard technical challenge, but tractable with sufficient effort and urgency.
6. **Geopolitical stakes are existential** — Superintelligence provides decisive military advantage; the free world must maintain its lead over authoritarian powers.
7. **Government involvement is inevitable** — By 2027/28, a Manhattan Project-scale government AGI project ("The Project") will emerge.
